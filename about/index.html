RESEARCH EXPERIENCES

Graduate Student, Social Computing Group,Web Sciences Center (09/2012 — present)
Awards:Graduate Research Fellowship, Scholarship forPostgraduate/Graduate Students
  • Analysis of News Diffusion in Recommender Systems: (funded by NSFC and Huawei) While using multidimensional vectors to characterize the user's interest, simulating with multi-agent model, and factoring the quality of the users and news into recommendation, we analyzed the structure of the users' network and the factors impacting on the recommendations and diffusion of news.
  • Mining in Large Scale Networks: (funded by Tencent) Used Graphlab —a high-level graph-parallel abstraction developed byCMU, and developed enhanced algorithms foranalysisin large scale network. I mainly worked on enhancing genetic algorithm for detecting communities and finding shortest paths in large scale networks.
  • Recommender Systems for Power-law Distributionof Users' Activity Frequency: (funded by NSFC and Huawei) We found that previous social recommendation methods lead to serious delay of information propagation since many users are connected to inactive leaders. To solve this problem, we designed a new similarity measure which takes into account users' activity frequencies. With this similarity measure, the average delay is significantly shortened and the recommendation accuracy is largely improved.
  • Link Prediction in Large Scale Networks: (funded by NSFC) Proposed an improved SVM model to predict binary relationship between users. Compared to the existing algorithms and Adaboost for all of them, my algorithm achieves higher accuracy with much less training data. With thousands of training samples, this algorithm can predict millions of relationships in highaccuracy.
  • Community Detection in Large Heterogeneous Networks: (funded by Tencent) Researched fast algorithms for community detection in large scale social networks, identify the network community structure combining with network topology and node attributes. Mined the theme of community, and predicted the trends of new topics in social networks.
  • Prediction of KPI in Large Communication Network: (funded by Huawei) Develop a generalized adaptive model (GAM) based on  high dimensional feature space and billions of samples. Also I am considering using higher dimensional features generated by Auto-encoder in Deep Neuro-network.
